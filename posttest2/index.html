Demon Slayer is an anime written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows the protagonist Tanjiro Kamado on his journey to restore his younger sister back to her human self. One fateful day Tanjiro, the eldest child, and man of the house, was in the nearest village working and brining in items the family needed. During his travel back home, he returned to his entire family mauled. Noticing his younger sister, Nezuko Kamado, still had a little life in her he rushes with her on his back to get help. Nezuko begins to act strange and it was then he noticed she is no longer human.
